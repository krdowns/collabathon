OUR STORY

hiker, /ˈhʌɪkə/, (noun): a person who walks for long distances, especially across country. Hiking Meetups is a way of life in which we hikers, get together to conquer life's biggest mountains. We value building relationships over crossing great terrains and scoping breath-taking landscapes.

We welcome hikers of all kinds of backgrounds and levels. Depending on your goal, we have a variation of groups you can join.
